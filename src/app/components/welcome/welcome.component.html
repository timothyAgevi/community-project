How it Works

As a learner...

You register your intention of the course you intend to study which is listed amongst the various course categories. The categories range from Business, IT and Software to Design, Photography and Video as well as Development course. Under each categories a available courses you can seek sponsorship for. Getting preferred course of study, You fill a form and submit.

As a Sponsor...

You browse through our list of available students seeeking sponsorship for particular course of study. Each students has an email and a button that links directly to the course page the student intends to study which is on Udemy. There you can gift them the course registering them with their email.
